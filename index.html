About the Persona Series

This website focuses on the Persona video game series developed by Atlus, a role-playing franchise that blends an everyday life simulation with supernatural elements. The site explores how the series uses themes of identity, self-discovery, and societal pressure, along with mythological symbolism, to create its narratives.
